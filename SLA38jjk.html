Main Characters

Satoru Gojo

The Strongest Jujutsu Sorcerer. Satoru Gojo is the strongest jujutsu sorcerer in the world and a central character in Jujutsu Kaisen, known for his Limitless technique and Six Eyes ability. Background and Role Satoru Gojo (五条 悟, Gojō Satoru) is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High, where he mentors students like Yuji Itadori and Yuta Okkotsu. He is the pride of the Gojo Clan and the first in 400 years to inherit both the Limitless technique and the Six Eyes, making him exceptionally powerful. Gojo’s influence extends beyond teaching; he actively protects allies and reshapes the jujutsu world’s balance.

Ryomen Sukuna

The King of Curses.Ryomen Sukuna (両りょう面めん宿すく儺な Ryōmen Sukuna?), more often referred to as simply Sukuna (宿すく儺な Sukuna?), is the main antagonist of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He was history's strongest jujutsu sorcerer, regarded as the undisputed King of Curses (呪のろいの王おう Noroi no Ō?). According to legend, Sukuna was an Imaginary Demon (仮か想そうの鬼き神じん Kasō no Kijin?) born over a thousand years ago during the Heian Era, the Golden Age of Jujutsu. In truth, he was an undefeated human sorcerer also known as the Disgraced One (堕だ天てん Daten?),[2] having felled countless other sorcerers that challenged him. After accepting a proposal from Kenjaku to participate in the Culling Game,[3] Sukuna continued existing after death by transforming himself into a cursed object, remaining in a state in which his cursed energy and soul was split into his twenty indestructible fingers.[1] Sukuna was incarnated in June 2018 when Yuji Itadori consumed one of his fingers.[4] He was trapped inside Yuji's body and was rarely in control until he managed to transfer his soul into Megumi Fushiguro's body.[5]
